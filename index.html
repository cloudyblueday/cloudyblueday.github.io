hey! i'm dylan. i'm 20, and finishing up my junior year at berklee college of music as a double major in film scoring and electronic production & design. this means i spend a lot of time learning every tricky witchcrafty tool to make the best music for your story possible, whether that be the orchestra, the modular synth, or me banging some pots and pans - but masterfully. when i'm not doing schoolwork, i'm scribbling some original music on my guitar, writing poetry, doodling on my ipad, or playing lots of dungeons & dragons. i don't just make music - i make stories that occupy every sense. let me show you how!
